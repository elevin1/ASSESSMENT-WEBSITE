    Members

    and Concept

    The main concept of NCT is to be an ever-expanding group with unlimited members from different regions of the world. Currently, there are 23 members ranging from their late teens to late twenties, consisting of Lee Taeyong, Moon Taeil, Johnny Seo, Nakamoto Yuta, Qian Kun, Kim Doyoung, Ten, Jeong Jaehyun, Winwin, Kim Jungwoo, Lucas, Mark Lee, Xiaojun, Hendery, Huang Renjun, Lee Jeno, Lee Haechan, Na Jaemin, Liu Yangyang, Osaki Shotaro, Jeong Sungchan, Zhong Chenle and Park Jisung. 13 out of the 23 members are foreigners, with Johnny being from America, Mark being Canadian, Yuta and Shotaro being Japanese, Ten being Thai and Kun, Winwin, Lucas, Xiaojun, Henry, Renjun, Yangyang and Chenle being Chinese.

    Projects

    Typically, NCT does music promotions in sub-units such as NCT Dream, NCT 127, WayV and NCT U. Along with the separate activities of each unit, the NCT group has combined twice to promote with full length albums, first with their 2018 release ‘NCT 2018 Empathy’ and then with their late 2020 two-part album ’NCT 2020 Resonance’, in parts 1 and 2.

    ‘NCT 2018 Empathy’ was the first studio album produced by NCT. Its title track ‘Black on Black’ consisted of all 18 members from 3 different units at the time, additionally introducing Kun, Jungwoo and Lucas to the NCT group. The album featured 14 different tracks of different styles from the time of debut in 2016 up to that point.

    ‘NCT 2020 Resonance’ was the second studio album produced by NCT. The project and title track ‘Resonance’ featured all 3 fixed units' current members, while also introducing 2 new members, Shotaro and Sungchan, to join NCT. The album marked growth and progression from ‘Empathy’, featuring tracks of all different genres and styles.

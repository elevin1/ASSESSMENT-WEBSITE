    • WayV

    Background

    WayV is the fourth sub-group of NCT, a South Korean K-Pop boy band under SM Entertainment’s Chinese sub-label, Label V. The group debuted in 2019 with Chinese and Thai members. The group itself was not promoted under NCT until the NCT 2020 project, instead promoting as a separate China-based boy group and producing primarily Chinese songs. They are well known for their strong familial group dynamic and sense of humour.

    Group Dynamic

    WayV consists of 7 members: Kun, Ten, Winwin, Xiaojun, Lucas, Hendery and Yangyang. Winwin first debuted in 2016 with NCT 127 before unofficially leaving to focus on WayV promotions. Kun and Lucas originally debuted in the NCT 2018 project. Xiaojun, Henry and Yangyang were introduced through the SM Rookies group in 2019 prior to debuting in WayV.

    Achievements

    WayV has been highly recognised throughout Asia as a K-pop group with a memorable and distinct style, with the group wanting to be recognised as Asian pop or their own style of ‘WayV pop’. Their world recognition grew with the English version of their release “Love Talk”. WayV have received multiple international and Asian awards, as well as many brand deals for their success.

    Album Success

    WayV’s 2019 English release “Love Talk” peaked at 3rd on the Billboard World Digital Song Sales chart, their highest ever international ranking. In March 2021, their third EP ‘Kick Back’ marked the group’s first number 1 album on the Gaon Album Chart, earning the group’s first album certification for over 250 000 sales in South Korea.

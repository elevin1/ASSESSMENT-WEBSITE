    Background

    NCT Dream is the third sub-group of NCT, a South Korean K-Pop boy band under the entertainment company SM Entertainment in 2016. The concept provided for them was one of youth and brightness, debuting in 2016 as the teen unit of NCT, with the average age of all the members being 15.6 years old at the time of debut. The group debuted with the single “Chewing Gum” on August 25, 2016.

    Group Dynamic

    As of 2021, NCT Dream consists of a lineup of 7 members: Mark, Renjun, Jeno, Haechan, Jaemin, Chenle and Jisung. Mark graduated from the group in 2018 due to the group’s original graduation system. However, at the end of 2020, the graduation system was abolished, with Mark returning as a member and NCT Dream becoming a fixed unit with the 7 original members.

    Achievements

    NCT Dream has been internationally recognised as one of the most influential and notable teenage artist groups of surrounding generations. They are the first and only Asian artists to have appeared three consecutive times on Billboard’s ’21 under 21’ list in 2018, 2019 and 2020 due to their impact on sales and social media in the musical industry in surrounding generations.

    Album Success

    The May 2021 release of their first studio album “Hot Sauce” in 2021 earnt the group the title of ‘double million sellers’, selling over 2,000,000 copies. The title track also won NCT Dream eight music show trophies, including a triple crown on M-Countdown. In June 2021, their repackage album ‘Hello Future’ won NCT Dream 6 music show awards. This release gained NCT Dream the title of youngest ever K-pop ‘triple million sellers’.
